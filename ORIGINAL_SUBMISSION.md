## Idea
Innovation Idea:
AGRIXMBD 2.0 is an AI-native Agricultural Intelligence Cloud that fuses ISRO satellite data (Cartosat, Resourcesat, RISAT) with real-time IoT telemetry to build a live Digital Twin of every farm. Its core engine, PFRIE, applies CNNs, Vision Transformers, and Graph Neural Networks to forecast soil moisture loss, pest outbreaks, and flash droughts up to 21 days in advance — a shift from reactive farming to predictive, twin-based intelligence.
Problem Solved:
Indian farmers, especially smallholders, lack early-warning tools and operate on information asymmetry — pest attacks, water stress, and climate shocks are usually detected only after visible crop damage, by which point losses are irreversible. Existing platforms offer static dashboards, not predictive, farm-specific intelligence, and rarely connect insights to real economic action.
Impact:
With 94.2% predictive accuracy, AGRIXMBD 2.0 enables farmers to act before damage occurs, reducing crop losses and input waste. Its AREX engine converts predictions into action — automating machinery booking, cold storage, drone deployment, and weather-indexed insurance — while a vernacular Voice AI ensures accessibility for non-technical rural users. Government dashboards extend this to regional food-security planning, aligning directly with Digital India's goal of technology-led rural transformation.
