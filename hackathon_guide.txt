--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. EXPANDED PROBLEM STATEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Smallholder farmers in India face severe yield losses, resource wastage, and financial 
exploitation due to three core systemic gaps:
1. **The Telemetry Gap (Space vs. Soil)**: Satellite imagery provides regional crop 
   visuals (macro scale) but lacks soil chemistry ground-truth details. Local soil 
   sensors provide local measurements (micro scale) but lack regional context. Fusing 
   space and soil data has historically been difficult.
2. **The Advisory Gap (Language & Accessibility)**: High-tech dashboards are built 
   in English and require complex computer literacy. Local farmers need access to 
   intuitive, voice-controlled advisors in their native regional languages (Hindi, Marathi).
3. **The Financial & Supply Chain Gap (Exploitation)**: Farmers lack secure 
   arrangements to sell yields. Middlemen exploit them, cold chain logistics are unmonitored 
   (causing 30% crop spoilage), crop insurance payouts take months of corrupt audits, and 
   carbon-offset credits are locked behind complex verifications.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. EXPANDED SOLUTION STATEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
AGRIXMBD 2.0 is an integrated, end-to-end **Agricultural Intelligence Cloud & Soil 
Digital Twin Portal** that addresses these gaps. It fuses ISRO Bhoonidhi STAC API 
satellite analytics with physical ESP32 IoT ground telemetry. 

By mapping these datasets into a thermodynamic Soil Digital Twin, the platform exposes 
micro-services: autonomous drone path planners, weather-indexed crop insurance, cold 
storage tracking, escrow contract ledgers, a voice-activated LLM advisor, and a direct 
Retailer-to-Farmer Agricultural E-Commerce Marketplace.

3. DETAILED MODULE-BY-MODULE BREAKDOWN
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### MODULE 1: FIELD GIS & REGIONAL RISK PORTAL (Tab: Field GIS & Risk)
* **What it does**: Provides a visual geospatial overview of registered farm plots.
* **Key Features**:
  - *Coordinates Registration*: Supports two input modes:
    - **Single Center Point Mode**: Separate input columns for Latitude, Longitude, and plot size in meters (converts to a closed square polygon automatically).
    - **Boundary Polygon Coords List**: Accepts custom coordinate lists.
  - *MapLibre GL Map*: Centers and zooms in on crop plots, highlighting them with green boundaries.
  - *Field Dials*: Real-time NPK levels and Soil moisture ratios.
  - *Pre-Visual Stress Diagnostics*: Predicts moisture and drought risks up to 21 days in advance.

### MODULE 2: THERMODYNAMIC SOIL DIGITAL TWIN (Tab: Soil Digital Twin)
* **What it does**: Simulates the physical thermodynamics of the soil per plot.
* **Key Features**:
  - *ISRO Bhoonidhi STAC Twin Synchronizer*: Queries LISS-IV multispectral data to overlay moisture index heatmaps on top of satellite imagery.
  - *AI Opacity Control Slider*: Allows farmers to adjust the overlay transparency to look through the heat map and view the physical terrain.
  - *Simulation Controls*: Sliders for Soil Moisture and Temperature that dynamically calculate:
    - **Soil Vapor Flux (mm/day)**: Water evaporation rate.
    - **Heat Flux Coefficient (W/m²)**: Thermal conductivity of the soil.
    - **NPK Absorption Efficiency (%)**: Nutrient transport capacity (drops under extreme heat/drought).

### MODULE 3: AREX MARKETPLACE & DECENTRALIZED ECONOMY (Tab: AREX Economy)
* **What it does**: Secures transaction trust and resource sharing.
* **Key Features**:
  - *AREX Escrow Ledger*: Locks buyer funds for crops. Payouts are automatically released only when telemetry quality parameters (protein, oil, moisture) are satisfied.
  - *Machinery Booking Marketplace*: Allows farmers to book drone sprayers, harvesters, or tractors.
  - *Logistics Cold Chain Queue*: Tracks regional cold storage vehicles, displaying real-time box temperature to prevent transit spoilage.

### MODULE 4: VOICE AI ADVISOR (Tab: Voice AI)
* **What it does**: A voice-activated chatbot for farmers.
* **Key Features**:
  - *Speech-To-Text*: Converts verbal questions in regional languages (Hindi, Marathi, English) using browser Speech Recognition.
  - *Voice Synthesis*: Reads LLM advisory responses aloud.
  - *Quick Prompts*: One-click buttons to check soil stress or market rates.

### MODULE 5: PFRIE CORE ANALYTICS (Tab: PFRIE Analytics)
* **What it does**: The analytical brain of the platform, composed of 6 ML sub-engines:
  1. *Living Root Intelligence™*: Estimates root zone biomass density.
  2. *Groundwater Digital Twin™*: Computes sub-surface water table recharge rates.
  3. *Village Disease Intelligence™*: Maps crop disease vectors based on local telemetry.
  4. *Climate Survival Simulator™*: Calculates local crop drought and heat tolerance.
  5. *Autonomous Seasonal Planner™*: Auto-schedules optimal foliar spray/fertilization times.
  6. *Farm Resilience Score™*: Combines indices into a bank-grade credit safety score.

### MODULE 6: SUSTAINABILITY LEDGER (Widget inside PFRIE tab)
* **What it does**: Manages carbon assets and insurance.
* **Key Features**:
  - *Weather-Indexed Crop Insurance*: Displays active policies and lets farmers file weather claims which are verified automatically against weather logs ( rainfall < 12mm).
  - *Carbon Credit Tracker*: Calculates verified CO2 offset metric tons and displays market value in INR (INR 1200 per ton).

### MODULE 7: RETAILER AGRI E-COMMERCE MARKETPLACE (Integrated)
* **What it does**: Connects farmers directly with input suppliers, skipping local middleman inflation.
* **Key Features**:
  - *Retailer Portal*: Allows vendors to list crop supplements, organic fertilizers ("khat"), hybrid seeds, and bio-pesticides.
  - *Farmer Procurement*: Farmers can purchase items directly using their AREX wallet.

### MODULE 8: EDITABLE FARMER PROFILE (Profile page)
* **What it does**: Manages farmer registration.
* **Key Features**:
  - *Profile Configuration*: Configures profile photo, full name, phone number, 12-digit Aadhar number, and land paper records.

4. IoT ESP32 SENSOR HUB CONFIGURATION & INTEGRATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* **Interface Protocol Options**:
  1. **WiFi Network**: Connects via TCP/IP (port 80) to the ESP32 server IP `192.168.4.1`.
  2. **Bluetooth BLE**: Connects via BLE GATT client subscribing to service UUID `0x4FAF`.
  3. **USB UART Serial**: Wired connection via `COM3`/`COM4` ports at `115200` baud.
* **Physical Sensors**:
  - *Capacitive Moisture Sensor v1.2*: Returns analog values corresponding to soil wetness.
  - *Modbus RS485 NPK Sensor*: Queries Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium in mg/kg.
  - *DHT22 sensor*: Returns temperature (°C) and humidity (%).
* **Serial Monitor**: Displays incoming JSON packets in real-time.
